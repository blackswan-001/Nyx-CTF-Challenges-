====================================================
  Hidden in Plain Sight
  Category : Reverse Engineering   |   Difficulty : Medium
====================================================

You found the signal in the noise.
Now you must learn that not everything visible is true.

A binary has been provided.
Run it.
Look inside it.
You will find what appear to be answers.

They are not answers.
They are a test of character.

The greedy will submit what they find first.
The disciplined will ask why nothing is printing.

There is one real answer.
It will not be handed to you.
It must be earned — by understanding what the program
actually does, not what it appears to contain.
