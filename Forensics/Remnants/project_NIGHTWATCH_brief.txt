----------------------------------------
PROJECT SUMMARY
----------------------------------------

NIGHTWATCH is Nyx Research's internal
anomaly detection and network monitoring
initiative, deployed organisation-wide
in Q3 2021.

Core functions:

  — Real-time traffic analysis across
    all internal network segments
  — Automated alerting on after-hours
    access to restricted directories
  — Badge access cross-referencing
    against system login records
  — Encrypted log retention for
    a minimum of 18 months

----------------------------------------
KNOWN LIMITATIONS
----------------------------------------

NIGHTWATCH does not monitor:

  — Encrypted USB transfer sessions
    initiated at physical terminals
  — Peer-to-peer relay communications
    outside the primary mail gateway
  — After-hours access when the
    duty officer override is active

These limitations have been flagged
to senior leadership as of 2025-07-14.
Remediation is pending budget approval.
